About me

My name is Sean, and I am the Associate Director of Biostatistics at RainCity Analytics, a health outcomes and economics research company based in Vancouver, Canada. I lead multidisciplinary teams across HEOR and biostatistics projects, overseeing work from study design through final delivery. My work spans real-world evidence generation, systematic literature reviews, meta-analyses, and survival and comparative effectiveness analyses across a range of disease areas, including cancer and rare diseases.

My background spans academia, public health, and industry, with roles at the BC Centre for Disease Control, UBC, and various global health initiatives. I have a passion for health research, epidemiology, statistical methods, and reproducible data science — particularly in data analysis, visualisation, and automation.
